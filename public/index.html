📄 User Guide — Getting Started

1
Login — Use username admin and password admin123 to access the CRM.
2
Add a Lead — Click the "+ Add Lead" button. Fill in Name, Email, Phone, Source and Notes. Click Save Lead.
3
Manage Lead Status — Use the action buttons: 📞 to mark Contacted, ✅ to mark Converted, ❌ to mark Lost, ✏️ to Edit, 🗑 to Delete.
4
Filter Leads — Use the filter pills (All, New, Contacted, Converted, Lost) to filter the table. Click metric cards to filter too.
5
Export Data — Click "📤 Export" to download all leads as a CSV file for Excel or Google Sheets.
6
View Reports — Go to Reports section to see charts, source breakdown and full lead report.
7
Integrations — Use the Integrations section to send WhatsApp messages or export to Google Sheets.
8
Dashboard — The Dashboard shows your win rate gauge, total leads, converted count and recent activity.

🎓 Training — Key Concepts

▶
Lead Pipeline: New → Contacted → Converted (Won) or Lost. Move leads through stages using action buttons.
▶
Win Rate: Percentage of leads that become Converted. Formula: (Converted ÷ Total Leads) × 100%.
▶
Lead Source: Where the lead came from — LinkedIn, Website, Referral, Cold Call, Email, Other. Track which sources perform best in Reports.
▶
Activity Log: Every action (add, edit, delete, status change) is recorded in the Activity tab for audit purposes.
▶
Contacts: All leads are stored as contacts. Use the search bar in Contacts to quickly find a specific person.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new lead? +
Click the "+ Add Lead" button in the top right or in the leads table. Fill in the required fields (Name and Email are mandatory) and click Save Lead.
How do I change a lead's status? +
Use the action buttons in the table: 📞 marks as Contacted, ✅ marks as Converted, ❌ marks as Lost. Or click ✏️ Edit and change the status dropdown.
How do I export leads? +
Click the "📤 Export" button in the top navigation. A CSV file will be downloaded with all lead data including Name, Email, Phone, Source, Status and Date.
What is the Win Rate? +
Win Rate is the percentage of total leads that have been Converted. It is calculated as: (Number of Converted Leads ÷ Total Leads) × 100.
How do I send a WhatsApp message to a lead? +
Go to Integrations → WhatsApp → Click "Send Message" → Enter the phone number and message → Click "Open WhatsApp". It will open WhatsApp Web with the message pre-filled.
Where is my data stored? +
All lead data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (cloud database). Your data is safe and accessible as long as the server is running.
